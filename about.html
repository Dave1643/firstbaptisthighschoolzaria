About Our School

Shaping leaders, building character, and inspiring excellence since 1965.

Our History

First Baptist High School Zaria was founded in 1965 with a vision to provide quality education rooted in discipline, moral values, and academic excellence. Over the years, the school has grown into one of the leading secondary institutions in Nigeria, producing graduates who excel in academics, leadership, and service.

With over 50 years of heritage, FBHSZ continues to uphold its mission of raising responsible citizens who will contribute positively to society and the nation.
